# AtlasBurn Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AtlasBurn is built for serious builders solving real problems.  
We are committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and focused environment for everyone interacting with this project.

This is not a casual or chaotic space.  
This is a system designed for clarity, execution, and high standards.

---

## Expected Behavior

All contributors and participants are expected to:

- Communicate with clarity and respect
- Stay focused on technical and product-related discussions
- Provide constructive feedback backed by reasoning
- Respect differing viewpoints while maintaining professionalism
- Think in terms of systems, not noise

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Low-effort or spam contributions
- Off-topic discussions that dilute focus
- Disrespectful or dismissive communication
- Attempts to disrupt the integrity of the project

---

## Enforcement

The maintainers of AtlasBurn reserve the right to:

- Remove or reject contributions that do not meet standards
- Block or restrict users engaging in harmful behavior
- Maintain strict quality control over all changes

---

## Philosophy

AtlasBurn is not just code.  
It is infrastructure.

We value:
- Precision over volume  
- Signal over noise  
- Execution over intention
